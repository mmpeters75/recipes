Hershey Bar Pie

Crust

  • 14 graham crackers
  • 1/2 cup melted butter
  • 1/4 cup powdered sugar
  • Mix well and press into pie plate

Filling

  • 6 Hershey bars
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 15 marshmallows
  • 1 cup whipping cream
  • 11/2 cup chopped nuts
  •  
    1. Put in pan
    2. Cook over low heat, stirring until dissolved
    3. Add hershey bars and stir until well blended
    4. Remove from fire and cool
    5. Whip cream and fold cool chocolate mixture
    6. Add finely chopped pecans if desired.
    7. Pour into shell and let cool several hours or overnight
