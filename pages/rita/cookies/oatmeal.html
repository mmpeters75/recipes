Oatmeal Cookies

  • 1 1/2 cup flour
  • 3/4 cup crisco
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 2 eggs beaten
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 2 cups oatmeal
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • raisins (optional)
  •  
    1. Cream together crisco, sugar, and eggs
    2. Add flour, soda, and spices with milk
    3. Add oatmeal and raisins
    4. Bake at 400° for 15 minutes
