Chocolate Nut Cookies

  • 1 box Pillsbury Supreme Lemon Cake Mix
  • 1/3 cup oil
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/4 cup sugar (to flatten cookies)
  •  
    1. Preheat oven to 375°
    2. Shape dough into 1" balls
    3. Place 2" apart on ungreased sheet
    4. Dip bottom of glass in sugar. Flatten cookies.
    5. Bake 5-7 minutes.
    6. Combine 1 cup powdered sugar and 2 to 3 tbsp lemon juice. Drizzle over cookies
